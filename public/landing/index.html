The Problem

THE RECRUITING PROCESS IS BROKEN.

Every family discovers it the hard way. You start Googling. You build a spreadsheet. You lose track of who you emailed. You find out three months later that a program's setter already graduated and nobody told you.


The information is out there — buried across hundreds of athletic department websites, outdated roster PDFs, and scattered Instagram DMs. Nobody gave athletes a real tool. Until now.

❌ The Spreadsheet Trap You start a Google Sheet. By week three it has 14 tabs, broken formulas, and you still don't know which schools have setter openings.
❌ Information Overload D-I, D-II, D-III, NAIA, JUCO — each division has different rules, timelines, scholarship structures, and coach contact norms. Nobody explains the map.
❌ The Ghost Email Problem You email a coach. Silence. You don't know if the program is recruiting your position, your grad year, or your region. It feels like shouting into the void.
❌ Academic Fit is an Afterthought Most families spend 90% of their time on athletic fit and 10% on whether the school actually has the major their athlete cares about. Then they visit campus and realize the mismatch.
❌ No Sense of Progress Without a system, every week feels like starting over. There's no clear view of where you stand with any program, who you've contacted, or what comes next.

The Recruiting Journey

HOW IT
ACTUALLY WORKS.

The Hub mirrors the real recruiting process — every stage mapped and tracked so nothing falls through the cracks.

1
Stage One
Build Your Target List
Start with 30-50 programs across divisions. Use the AI Discovery Engine to find schools that match your position need, grad year window, academic interests, and geography — not just the ones you've heard of.
2
Stage Two
Submit Questionnaires
Every school has a recruiting questionnaire. The Hub links directly to each one and tracks which ones you've submitted. This is step one in making yourself visible to coaching staffs.
3
Stage Three
Reach Out to Coaches
Use the outreach templates built for setters. Know the coach's name, email, and whether they've been winning or losing lately before you hit send. Check the Local Contact Radar to see if a current player on the roster shares your home region.
4
Stage Four
Coach Contact & Dialogue
A coach responds. The Hub tracks every program that enters this stage. Review the Academic Fit Profile together as a family before the next call — walk in knowing the school, not just the team.
5
Stage Five
Campus Visits
You're getting serious. The Hub helps you track which campuses you've visited, what your impression was, and where the program stands on scholarship or walk-on conversations.
★
Stage Six
Offers & Decision
The moment everything has been building toward. The Hub surfaces every offer, every program you're weighing — academic fit, athletic fit, scholarship, culture — all in one view to make the call with clarity.
